What is akiba plus?
Akiba plus helps you take your savings to the next level!

In our innovative savings model, you enjoy unparalleled flexibility to customize your savings plans based on your preferences and financial aspirations.

Whether opting for a normal or more dynamic aggressive strategy, you can specify a fixed amount you wish to save daily, weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly.

Aggressive strategy

With the aggressive strategy, savings amount multiplies by the corresponding day, week, or month. This feature allows you to witness accelerated growth in your savings, providing a unique and dynamic approach to reaching your financial goals.

Try out the comprehensive calculator to to plan and achieve your desired financial outcomes.
